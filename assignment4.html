Challenge 5: Accessability Concerns

There are a couple of accessability concerns I have regarding the form I have created. The first concern I have is that the text is quite small and can be difficult to read for someone who is visually impared in some way. My main concern, however, is the range slider. Clicking and dragging is difficult for many people with motor issues. Disregarding the fact that I put it there as a joke, the issue of sliders like this is still present. I think possibly making a text box where the user can type the desired input could be a possible solution to this problem.
